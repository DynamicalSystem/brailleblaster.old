Here are the requirements for the BrailleBlaster wordprocessor/editor.

Typewriter-like font with italic, bold and underline variants. 

A Unicode Braille font that shows dot patterns.

Ability to show emphasis and highlighting of single characters and 
words. 

Closely tied to position in xml parse tree. Scrolling and moving the 
cursor must move around in the tree.

Ability to change contrast.

Ability to delete elements with their subtrees.

Ability to add elements with their subtrees and with the correct name 
and attributes.
